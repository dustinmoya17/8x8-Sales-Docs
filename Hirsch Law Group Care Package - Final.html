🎯 Solution Architecture

Your 8x8 Environment

A unified platform combining 30 contact center agents (X6 licenses with Quality Management & Speech Analytics) and 30 phone users (X2 licenses) with Microsoft Teams integration and Clio connectivity.

📞 Contact Center (X6)

Inbound Voice Queues

Skills-based routing with queue callback, wait time announcements, and backup queue overflow. Route Spanish and Hungarian calls to specialized agents.

Supervisor Workspace

Real-time dashboards with queue summaries, agent status, and performance metrics. Move agents between queues, monitor live calls, whisper coach, or barge in.

Call Analytics

Cradle-to-grave visibility showing every call event—IVR navigation, queue placement, agent handling, holds, transfers, and exactly who ended the call.

Transaction Codes

Capture why clients called with disposition codes. Force agents to complete codes before taking another call—no more gaps in your data.

🎙️ Quality Management & Speech Analytics

AI Summarization

Automatic call summaries describing what the call was about, key points discussed, and action items identified—no manual note-taking required.

Sentiment Analysis

Evaluate calls based on overtalk, agent clarity, and custom criteria. Automatically flag calls needing attention based on your defined thresholds.

Full Transcription

Complete call transcripts searchable by keyword or phrase. Create topic categories for communication barriers, customer experience issues, or compliance monitoring.

Evaluation Tools

Build custom scorecards, take timestamped notes during playback, and send recording snippets directly to agents for coaching conversations.

📊 100% Call Recording

All contact center calls recorded automatically with no agent control to disable. Recordings stored for 30 days included, with options to extend to 1, 3, 5, or 10 years as needed.

💼 Unified Communications (X2)

SMS & Fax

Send and receive text messages and faxes directly from Teams. Outbound faxes up to 40 pages, inbound faxes copied to multiple email addresses.

Mobile App

Full-featured mobile application for attorneys on the go. Make calls showing firm number, access voicemail, and SMS from anywhere.

Voicemail Transcription

Voicemails transcribed and delivered to email. Choose attachment with transcription, notification only, or access directly within Teams.
